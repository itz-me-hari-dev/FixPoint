About FixPoint

On-Demand Home Service Booking Platform

Smart & Reliable Home Services

FixPoint connects customers with trusted service providers like plumbers, electricians, painters, and mechanics based on location and availability.

Customers can search, book services online, and make secure payments. Providers manage bookings while the admin ensures service quality.

How It Works

Search Services

Find providers by category and location.

Book Service

Select date and service hours to confirm booking.

Complete & Pay

Service completed with secure online payment.

Platform Roles

Customers

Search, book, track services, and make payments.

Service Providers

Manage profiles, accept bookings, and complete services.

Admin

Approve providers and monitor the entire platform.
